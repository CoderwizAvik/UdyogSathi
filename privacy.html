Privacy Policy

Protecting your data and trust is our highest priority.

1. Introduction and Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how UdyogSathi ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our platform and services. By using UdyogSathi, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Last updated: October 18, 2025

2. Information We Collect

Personal Data

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for an account, post a job, or apply for a position. This may include:

  • **Authentication Data:** User IDs, email addresses (if provided), and temporary session tokens (managed by Firebase).
  • **Profile Data (Voluntary):** Name, contact number, employment history, skills, and current location.
  • **Content Data:** Any content you create, such as job descriptions, public messages, or job application details.

Usage and Technical Data

When you access the platform, our service providers (like Firebase) may automatically collect certain information that your browser or device sends, such as:

  • IP address, device type, browser type, and operating system.
  • Time and date of your visit, and pages viewed.
  • Data about your interactions with the platform (e.g., clicks, time spent on the app).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • **To Provide and Maintain the Service:** To operate the UdyogSathi platform, including authenticating users and managing data storage (using Firebase Firestore).
  • **To Connect Users:** To match job seekers with relevant job postings and vice-versa.
  • **Communication:** To send you service updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • **Security:** To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • **Improvement:** To analyze usage patterns and troubleshoot issues to improve the functionality and user experience of our application.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your Personal Data to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • **With Other Users:** When you apply for a job, your profile data is shared with the potential employer. When you post a job, your contact information may be shared with applicants.
  • **Service Providers:** We rely on Google's **Firebase** for backend services, including Authentication, Firestore database, and possibly analytics. Their handling of data is governed by their own policies.
  • **Legal Requirements:** If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).

5. Data Security and Retention

Data Security

We use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, leveraging the security infrastructure provided by **Firebase**. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Retention

We will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. This means we keep your profile and public content as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will initiate the removal of your data, subject to legal obligations.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your Personal Data:

  • **The Right to Access:** You have the right to request copies of your Personal Data.
  • **The Right to Rectification:** You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • **The Right to Erasure:** You have the right to request that we erase your Personal Data, under certain conditions.
  • **The Right to Withdraw Consent:** Where we rely on consent to process your Personal Data, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.
